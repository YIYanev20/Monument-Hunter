Objective: Defeat each country's guardian to collect its fragment. With that you will be able to beat BITler! To go to each country just click on its name.

Welcome to Monument Hunter! This is the introduction, so if you have already played the game just click "Skip".

Not long ago someone used ancient artifacts to revive Hitler. They succeeded in creating BITler instead. To beat him you will have to use those same artifacts on his weakened body.

But finding them won't be easy. They are hidden in monuments scattered around a couple of European countries. They are also guarded by Monument Guardians.

You will have to beat all Monument Guardians to get all fragments. But that's not all. Each monument is locked with a puzzle. They are all unique and reference the country's history.

Your journey starts now Monument Hunter!
